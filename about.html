Born from the Sea

The Mariner’s Chest is the ultimate subscription box for sailors, built by a passionate crew of seafarers and adventurers. We’ve spent years testing gear on open waters, curating practical tools, and developing a monthly experience tailored to life at sea.

Our Mission

To empower sailors with reliable gear, survival tools, maritime snacks, and expert resources – all delivered monthly. We aim to inspire confidence, preparedness, and joy with every voyage.

Our Subscribers

From novice cruisers to seasoned captains, we serve weekend warriors, offshore explorers, and competitive racers. Every box adapts to your lifestyle and evolves with your sailing goals.

What’s Inside

  • ✔ Durable, field-tested sailing gear
  • ✔ Emergency tools & navigation accessories
  • ✔ Healthy snacks for long passages
  • ✔ Digital sailing guides & exclusive content
  • ✔ Curated themes matched to your sailing style

Why Choose Us?

We’re not just a box – we’re a crew. When you subscribe to The Mariner’s Chest, you join a community of sailors who care about craftsmanship, safety, and exploration. We test every item ourselves, and we only ship what we would bring on board.
